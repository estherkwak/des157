Source: Kaur, Rupi. Milk and Honey. Kansas City, MO: Andrews McMeel, 2015. Web.
if you are not enough for yourself
you will never be enough
for someone else

-rupi kaur
what is stronger
than the human heart
which shatters over and over
and still lives

-rupi kaur
what’s the greatest lesson a woman should learn?

that since day one. she’s already had everything
she needs within herself. it’s the world that
convinced her she did not.

-rupi kaur
she is water

soft enough
to offer life
tough enough
to drown it away

-rupi kaur
the world
gives you
so much pain
and here you are
making gold out of it

there is nothing purer than that –rupi kaur
